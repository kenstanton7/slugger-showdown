Game Information & Rules

The Big Fly: Slugger Showdown is a high-stakes trivia game testing your knowledge of the greatest hitters in modern baseball history (2000-2026).

The Objective: Out of the two players shown, click on the one who has hit more career Home Runs. If you guess correctly, your streak increases. If you are wrong, you receive a strike.

Data & Accuracy: We pull live data directly from the MLB Stats API. Career totals include all regular-season games up to today. This means if a current player hits a home run in tonight's game, the numbers will update automatically!

The Pool: Our database includes nearly 1,000 players who have hit at least 50 home runs and played between the years 2000 and 2026.
